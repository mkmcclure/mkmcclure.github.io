Broadcasting Experience


WMUC Sports (2018-Present)

WMUC Sports is the student-run radio station at the University of Maryland. The station broadcasts all major-sport home games as well as the road games in the immediate area and some beyond if needed. Broadcasters are placed on crews for different sports and broadcast them throughout the season. I have broadcast men and women's soccer, basketball and lacrosse as well as football, wrestling and field hockey


Hamilton Joes Baseball Team (2019-2020)

The Hamilton Joes are a baseball team located in Hamilton, Ohio. They are a member of the Great Lakes Summer Collegiate Baseball League and compete from June to August. I spent the summer broadcasting 39 games and working as the PA announcer for 3. I also wrote game recaps, recorded commericals, hosted a podcast and helped update the hardware the team used to make their broadcasts easier to manage. I was slated to return for the 2020 season, but it was cancelled due to COVID-19.


Maryland Baseball Network (2021-Present)

The Maryland Baseball Network is the student-run radio station that broadcast all of the University of Maryland's baseball games throughout the season, including road games.


Great Bend Bat Cats (2021)

The Great Bend Bat Cats are a baseball team located in Great Bend, Kansas. They compete in the Sunflower Collegiate Baseball League from June until August. I will be the first intern in team history and am helping them get their broadcasting operation off the ground.

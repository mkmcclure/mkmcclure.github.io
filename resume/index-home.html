I'm a native of Glenmoore, Pennsylvania, a small suberb outside of Philadelphia. Working in sports was something that interested me from the time I was a teenager. My dad, brother and I used to do mock pre and post-game shows on our way to and from games. Those little shows turned into weekly NFL previews and even a few small podcast like shows. Those shows embedded a love of sports and sports broadcasting in me that fuels me to this day.
